Our Programs

Five Amazing Camp Experiences

Every week features all five programs — so kids get a full range of skills, creativity, and fun.

🎨
Monday Kickoff

Creative Kickoff

Dive into a week of imagination! Kids explore art, music, storytelling, and hands-on creative projects that bring out their inner artist and spark lifelong creativity.

Kids develop

  • Self-expression through art and storytelling
  • Fine motor skills and crafting techniques
  • Creative problem-solving mindset
  • Confidence in sharing their work
🔬
Tuesday Explorer

Science & Discovery

Real experiments, real discoveries! From slime labs to erupting volcanoes, kids get hands-on with science in ways school never quite manages to pull off.

Kids develop

  • Scientific thinking and observation skills
  • Hypothesis testing and reasoning
  • Genuine curiosity and love of discovery
  • STEM foundations for future learning
🎤
Wednesday Stage

Public Speaking

The most life-changing skill a child can develop. Through games, skits, debates and storytelling, kids learn to communicate with confidence and presence.

Kids develop

  • Confidence speaking in front of groups
  • Clear and structured communication
  • Active listening and empathy
  • Leadership presence and poise
🏃
Thursday Energy

Indoor Active Fun

Games, relays, team challenges, and physical activities that get hearts pumping and build teamwork in a safe, supervised indoor environment.

Kids develop

  • Physical fitness and coordination
  • Sportsmanship and fair play
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Healthy habits and love of movement
🎉
Friday Celebration

Theme Party Fridays

The highlight of every week! Themed celebrations featuring costumes, games, special activities, and unforgettable memories. Every Friday is a full-on party.

Kids develop

  • Cultural awareness and creativity
  • Social skills and community spirit
  • Joy, excitement and camp memories
  • Belonging and group identity

A Day at Camp

Your Child's Perfect Day

Structured enough to be productive, flexible enough to be genuinely fun. Here's a typical day.

9:30 AM

Morning Welcome 🌅

Drop-off, attendance and an energizing circle to kick off the day with excitement and intention.

9:45 AM

Creative Session 🎨

The main themed activity — art, science, drama or team challenge depending on the day of the week.

11:30 AM

Skills Workshop 🧠

Focused learning time where kids develop specific skills tied to the week's theme.

12:30 PM

Lunch & Free Time 🥪

Kids eat their packed lunch and enjoy free play, social time, or quiet activities of their choice.

1:15 PM

Active Games 🏃

Physical games, relays and team challenges that burn energy and build friendships.

2:30 PM

Group Project 👥

Collaborative activities where campers work together on something bigger than themselves.

3:30 PM

Wrap-Up & Reflection 💛

Daily sharing circle, celebrating wins and achievements, and getting ready for parent pick-up.

4:00 PM

Parent Pick-Up 🐝

Happy, tired, and full of stories to share — see you tomorrow!
